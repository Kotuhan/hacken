Token sale structure

Burning Haken model

Hacken uses revolutionary burning model for the investors equity growth, instead of traditional profit share or buy-back schemes.

Hacken uses revolutionary burning model for the investors equity growth, instead of traditional profit share or buy-back schemes.

All sales at HackenProof Marketplace and Hacken Accelerator are made in Hackens only. All profits from the operations earned are split in half. Half of the Hackens is deactivated and removed from the system and the other half is send back for sale at the open market.

This model leads for long run investment attractiveness by disbalancing Hackens supply and demand.

Hacken Ecosystem development would lead to Hacken demand increase from the Clients and VC at the same time there would be less Hackens available due to its constant deactivation. Less investors will want to sell their Hackens which will permanently increase Hackens market price.

Per mathematical calculations such model will lead to higher equity growth comparing with traditional profit share due to allocation of the profits from burned Hackens to long run investors.
